Adventure Sports In India

1.White Water Rafting

Many claim the first river rafting experience in Ganga at Rishikesh to be the beginning of an addiction! Located in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand, Rishikesh is one of the best places for White Water River Rafting in India.

  1. Best Season To Visit: Mid September to Mid June
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 1500 to INR 2500

2.Scuba Diving

New to many, Scuba diving is a rapidly growing water activity in India. Thanks to the vast Indian coastline housing numerous beautiful corals and ample marine life to be explored, this water adventure can prove to be both therapeutic and adventurous.

  1. Best Season: All Year, Except Monsoon Season
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 3500 - INR 5000

View More

3.Trekking

Right from rigorous professional treks to an easy hilltop or fort treks, India is a trekker-hub! With the abundance of nature and innumerable opportunities around it, India offers some of the best terrains to trekkers.

  1. Best Season: March - June
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 8500 - INR 30,000

4.Bungee Jumping

Visit Rishikesh for the most exhilarating Bungee experience in India. Located at the banks of Ganga in Uttarakhand, Rishikesh provides adventure with thrills of the highest order.

  1. Best Season: February - March & August - October
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 1800- INR 3500

5.Paragliding

With self as well as guided Paragliding sessions available in many states across the country, you can now conquer the loads of cash. Live your fantasy of being able to fly with all the safety and ease at one of the best locations for Paragliding.

  1. Best Season: October and mid-June
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 3500- INR 5500

6.Skydiving

Skydiving is a popular adventure sport on the majority of adventure seeker's list. This action-packed sport involves you falling from thousands of feet in the sky and landing with the help of a parachute.

  1. Best Season: All year round (except Monsoon season)
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 25,000 - INR 30,000

7.Flyboarding

Flyboarding has recently entered the world of adventure sports in India and has already gained a lot of popularity. With something new on the radar, adventure and latest addition and is quite popular among the tourists.

  1. Best Season: October - April
  2. Approx. Cost: INR 2500 - INR 3500

8.Flying Fox/Zip Lining

Want to try flying like Superman? Or flying in the air in super speed over the deep valley? Well, then you should Zip Lining. Ziplining, popularity has grown incredibly over a few years and is one of the most popular adventure sports in India.

  1. Best Season: September to November, January - March
